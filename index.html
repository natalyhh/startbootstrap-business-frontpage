About us

TOUCHSKY is a new division within UNIMAGNA METALÚRGICA LTDA. focused in solar business.

We believe in the catalyzing power of technology, empowering people to reach beyond and generating long lasting value for society.

This division is born under the challenge of aggregating technology in our production chain and under the untapped resource that is our tropical sun.

Our long term goal is to produce solar technologies in Brazil through international cooperations and local developments.

We are looking for technology partners interested in entering the Brazilian market.

Brazilian market highlights

+ Daily solar radiance: 4.500 to 6.300 Wh/m² (Atlas Brasileiro de Energia Solar). 5th place in world solar radiance potential.

+ By 2040, PV rooftop in Brazil is expected to account for 20% of the country electricity (Bloomberg New Energy Outlook 2017)

+ PV is still in its early days in Brazil and has a great prospect of development. The landscape is being set and it is still possible to benefit from a first-mover advantage.

+ Climate change is making hydropower, Brazil main energy resource (61%) an unreliable source.

+ PV has entered the country political agenda, with the Brazilian Ten Year Energy Expansion Plan issued in July 2017 projecting 13GW of solar installed capacity by 2026.

+ Recent regulation of the market has set a positive framework for the sector to operate in a healthy and self-sustained way.

+ Good receptivity of the brazilian market to new and green technologies.
