About us

TOUCHSKY is a new division within UNIMAGNA METALÚRGICA LTDA. focused in solar business.

We believe in the catalyzing power of technology in the development of a nation, empowering people to reach beyond and generating long lasting value for the society.

This division is born under the challenge of aggregating more technology in our production chains and under the untapped resource that is our Sun.

Our long term goal is to produce solar technologies in Brazil through international cooperations and local development.
